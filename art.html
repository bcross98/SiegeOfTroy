According to the Iliad, the Trojan War began when the Trojan prince Paris ran away to troy with Helen, wife of the Spartan king Menelaus. Paris had been chosen to judge which Greek goddess was the most beautiful. Aphrodite told him that were he to select her, she would give him love of the most beautiful woman on earth. He did so, and the love goddess made Helen fall in love with him, igniting the conflict and giving her the name "the face that launched a thousand ships."
